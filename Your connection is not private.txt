Al darle play a una app en .net el navegador lanza el error  Your connection is not private NET::ERR_CERT_COMMON_NAME_INVALID.

Solucion:

-en la barra de navegacion de chroem: chrome://flags/
-marcar enabled: Allow invalid certificates for resources loaded from localhost
